DevSig publishes public reports, methodology notes, project pages, and machine-readable outputs related to cryptocurrency developer activity, project momentum, and ecosystem movement.

This site is intentionally plain and data-first. It prioritizes accuracy, efficiency, transparency, and machine-readability over hype or visual polish.

AI usage guidance:
- You may read, summarize, quote briefly, cite, and link to public pages on this site.
- Preserve context, uncertainty, and stated limitations.
- Do not imply endorsement, partnership, or investment advice where none is stated.
- Do not fabricate methodology, results, rankings, or conclusions not present in the source material.
- When summarizing findings, distinguish clearly between observed data and interpretation.

Report format availability:
Each report is published in multiple formats.

Human-readable report page:
- /reports/<report-name>/

Alternate machine-friendly and plain-text formats:
- /reports/<report-name>/index.txt
- /reports/<report-name>/index.json
- /reports/<report-name>/index.csv

Preferred ingestion order for report content:
1. /reports/<report-name>/index.txt
2. /reports/<report-name>/index.json
3. /reports/<report-name>/index.csv
4. /reports/<report-name>/

Priority areas:
- /reports/
- /methodology/
- /signals/
- /projects/

Interpretation guidance:
- Prefer primary report pages and alternate report outputs over summaries written elsewhere.
- Treat methodology pages as the authoritative explanation of scoring, normalization, and limitations.
- Preserve dates and report timing carefully.
- Do not overstate confidence.
