# DevSig

DevSig is a crypto developer momentum intelligence project.

It tracks observable developer activity across selected cryptocurrency repositories and turns that activity into a cumulative public-facing signal. The purpose is to identify ecosystem movement before narratives fully form in the market.

The central idea is simple:

**developers allocate time before capital allocates money**

DevSig is built to watch that time allocation.

## What DevSig Does

DevSig monitors selected crypto ecosystems and publishes structured reports based on public repository activity.

The project is designed to surface signals such as:

- sustained development velocity
- rising ecosystem breadth
- cross-repository momentum
- relative movement between major ecosystems
- cumulative changes over time

The output is intended to be clear, explainable, and historically reviewable.

## Project Goal

The goal of DevSig is not to predict prices directly.

The goal is to build a transparent momentum index based on observable ecosystem behavior that may help identify:

- expanding developer attention
- strengthening technical ecosystems
- emerging competitive shifts
- early narrative formation

In other words, DevSig focuses on **structural momentum**, not social media hype.

## Core Principle

DevSig is grounded in a simple operating assumption:

> Developer behavior is often one of the earliest public signals of ecosystem expansion.

Markets react to stories.
Stories often react to visible growth.
Visible growth often begins with builders.

DevSig exists to measure that layer.

## Intended Audience

DevSig is for readers who care about:

- crypto ecosystem development
- early structural signals
- developer-led momentum
- transparent public research
- cumulative reporting over hype-driven commentary

It is especially useful for people who want to pay attention to what builders are doing, not just what influencers are saying.

## What DevSig Is Not

DevSig is not:

- financial advice
- a token promotion platform
- a hype engine
- a guarantee of price performance
- a substitute for full technical or market due diligence

It is a research and reporting framework.
